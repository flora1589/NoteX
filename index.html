About NoteX

India's First Gamified Learning Platform

"Padhai ko banaya hai game, ab learning hai easy aur fun!"

🚀 Our Story

NoteX was born from a simple frustration: why is studying so boring? As computer science students, we spent countless hours searching for good notes, struggling with complex concepts, and feeling demotivated by traditional learning methods.

That's when we had our "eureka" moment - what if learning could be as addictive as gaming? What if students could earn XP for studying, unlock badges for achievements, and compete with friends in a healthy, educational environment?

We combined the best of social learning, gamification, and AI technology to create NoteX - a platform where students don't just consume content, they actively participate, contribute, and grow together.

Today, NoteX serves over 5,000+ students across 200+ colleges in India, making learning more engaging, accessible, and fun for everyone.

🎯 Our Vision

To revolutionize education in India by making learning as engaging and addictive as the best mobile games, while building the largest community of student contributors.

🎮 Our Mission

To gamify education through XP systems, badges, and community challenges while providing high-quality, peer-reviewed study materials accessible to every student.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I earn XP on NoteX?

You can earn XP by uploading notes (+50-200 XP), completing quizzes (+10-50 XP), helping other students (+25 XP), and completing weekly quests (+75-300 XP).

Is NoteX free to use?

Yes! NoteX is completely free for students. We believe education should be accessible to everyone. Premium features may be introduced in the future, but core functionality will always remain free.

How can my college partner with NoteX?

We'd love to partner with your college! Email us at partnerships@notex.in with details about your institution, and we'll set up a call to discuss collaboration opportunities.

When will the AI Assistant be available?

Our AI Assistant is currently in beta testing with select users. We plan to roll it out to all users by the end of this semester. Join our Telegram community for early access updates!
